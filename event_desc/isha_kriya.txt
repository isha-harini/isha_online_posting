In this free session you will learn Isha Kriya, a simple yet powerful 12 minute practice, to help you attain health, clarity, and joy. Designed by Sadhguru, a realized yogi, this practice can help you become meditative effortlessly. Isha Kriya does not require any previous experience with meditation and can be done sitting in a chair. 
Sessions will be led by a trained instructor, after which ongoing online support will be available. 

This session is offered by Isha foundation, an international non-profit that aims to bring well-being to everyone through yoga and meditation. Ages 12 and up.
